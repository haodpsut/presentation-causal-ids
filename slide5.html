Our Contributions

A Novel Causal Framework

We design a novel, two-phase IDS framework (Causal-IDS) that separates causal graph discovery from the learning of its functional mechanisms.

The Importance of Non-Linearity

We demonstrate that accurately modeling complex, non-linear causal relationships is critical, as a naive linear causal model completely fails.

Superior Performance

We show that our enhanced Causal-IDS (GBoost) significantly outperforms standard anomaly detection baselines on the CIC-IDS2017 dataset.
