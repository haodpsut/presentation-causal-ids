Conclusion

A Paradigm Shift to Causal Reasoning

We introduced Causal-IDS, a framework that shifts from correlational anomaly detection to identifying violations of learned causal mechanisms, providing a more robust signal for intrusions.

Superior & Interpretable Performance

Our enhanced Causal-IDS (using non-linear models) proved highly effective, achieving a superior AUC of 0.84 and offering inherent interpretability by pinpointing which causal "law" was broken.

Future Direction

This causal approach paves the way for a new class of more robust, interpretable, and trustworthy intrusion detection systems.
