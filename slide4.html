A New Paradigm: "Don't Find Outliers, Find Lawbreakers"

The Core Idea

An attack is not just a statistical anomaly. It's an external intervention that violates the fundamental cause-and-effect "laws" of a normal network.

How Causal-IDS Works

  • Learn the "Laws": First, it learns a Structural Causal Model (a graph of cause-effect relationships) from purely benign traffic.
  • Check for Violations: For new traffic, it calculates a Causal Anomaly Score (CAS)—how badly the traffic deviates from the learned causal laws.
  • Detect Attacks: A high CAS indicates a mechanism has been broken, signaling a likely intrusion.
